Schmidt Happens Fantasy Baseball

Est. 2008

Welcome to the league! If you’re reading this, you’re either a member of the best fantasy baseball league in existence or your web crawlers are very thorough. Either way, welcome to Schmit Happens.

I’ve had the pleasure of being the commissioner of this league since its inception in 2008. Schmidt Happens has arguably been the most consistent thing in my life over that time.

A lot has changed in the now 16-year history of Schmidt Happens. We’ve had more than 30 fantasy managers, some of whom have been lost to history; we’ve been anywhere from a six to 12-team league; at one point we were even a two-catcher league (what were we thinking?) We’ve tried and failed many times to become a dynasty league. The coronavirus pandemic certainly didn't help with that.

But what’s remained is that this group is easily the most involved, dedicated baseball fans I know (except Eli… and obviously our robot team), and I couldn’t think of anyone I’d rather compete against every year. I have big plans for this league going forward (like finally being a dynasty league), and I hope Schmidt can continue to Happen for another 16+ years.

Sincerely,

Joe

Commissioner, 5x Champion
